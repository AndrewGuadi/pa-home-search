Our Story

From a small local brokerage to Central Pennsylvania’s most trusted real estate partner.

Humble Beginnings

My PA House Search started in 2010 when founder Ruth Koup recognized that homebuyers and sellers needed more than just listings—they needed guidance, transparency, and local expertise. What began as a one-woman consultancy in a shared office has grown into a full-service team at Keller Williams Realty, serving families across Harrisburg, Mechanicsburg, Lancaster, and beyond.

Early on, Ruth’s mission was simple: treat every client like family. Sales were modest, but word-of-mouth referrals fueled steady growth. By 2012, the team had helped over 50 households find or sell homes, establishing a reputation for personalized service and community focus.
