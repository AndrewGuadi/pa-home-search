About Us

At My PA House Search, we’re more than real estate agents—we’re your neighbors, advocates, and trusted guides through every step of buying or selling in Central Pennsylvania.

Our Mission

Founded in 2010 by Ruth Koup, My PA House Search was built on the belief that every client deserves white-glove service, transparent guidance, and deep local expertise. From first homes to luxury estates, our goal is to simplify the process, maximize your results, and build lifelong relationships.

Over the past decade we’ve grown from a solo consultancy to a full Keller Williams team, closing over 500 transactions across Harrisburg, Mechanicsburg, Lancaster, Carlisle, York, and beyond. Yet our core values—integrity, empathy, excellence, and community—remain as strong as ever.
