Top Buying Tips

  • Get Pre‑Approved First

    Secure financing before you shop to strengthen your offer and focus your search.

  • Research Neighborhoods

    Compare schools, commute times, and long‑term value before deciding.

  • Inspect Thoroughly

    Hire a qualified inspector to uncover any hidden issues early.

Top Selling Tips

  • Stage Strategically

    Highlight key rooms and create an emotional connection for buyers.

  • Price Competitively

    Analyze recent comps and set a price that draws traffic and multiple offers.

  • Market Aggressively

    Leverage professional photos, video tours, and targeted online ads.
